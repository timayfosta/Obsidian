# Campaign Overview: Shadows of Eldrath Isle

This redesigned D&D 5e campaign (2014 core rules) for **4 level 1 adventurers** (progressing to level 5) now spans **12-16 immersive sessions**, delving deeper into the misty, perilous heart of Eldrath. Expanded to **8 detailed acts**, it paces the journey like a fog-shrouded odyssey: slow-build survival, village intrigues, wilderland trials, ancient secrets, and a cataclysmic core confrontation. Themes of **self-discovery amid corruption**, **nature's fragile balance**, and **found family** shine through richer roleplay, environmental storytelling, and personalized "awakenings" (latent powers manifesting via mists).

Eldrath's mists amplify magic erratically (e.g., Wild Magic surges on spellcasting rolls of 1), birthing Blight horrors—twisted flora/fauna leaking Shadowfell ichor. Encounters scale for 4 PCs (DMG guidelines: easy-medium for immersion, deadly bosses). Use milestones for leveling. Immersive tools: Ambient mist effects (disadvantage on sight-based checks in fog), fey whispers (random prophecies), Heartstone shards (glowing quest beacons).

**Recurring Elements:**

- **Allies:** Lirael (elven herbalist/Veil Keeper, druid NPC), Sylva (wood elf scout ally).
- **Foes:** Blight beasts, cultists, Void Whisperer (shadow demon lord).
- **Loot Progression:** Herbal remedies → Fey relics → Heartstone shards (boons like _speak with animals_ 1/day).

#### 8-Act Structure: The Mistwalker's Path

##### Act 1: Shattered Awakening (Level 1)

**Immersive Hook:** Shipwreck interactivity (as prior read-aloud). Party washes ashore battered/amnesiac.

- **Survival Scavenge:** Tide pools yield rations, a rusted lantern (Survival DC 12 group challenge: 3 successes before 3 failures). Blighted stirges ambush (CR 1/8 x6; blood drain + poison fatigue).
- **Lirael's Tale:** Post-combat dialogue; she reveals Eldrath as Feywild-Toril nexus, Blight as Void incursion. Grants healing poultice (cure 1d8 HP).
- **First Steps:** Mist-veiled path to a hidden cove shrine (Perception DC 13: ancient runes grant inspiration). **Level Up:** None yet. **Treasure:** Lirael's thorn dagger (+1 piercing, poison 1d4).

##### Act 2: Whispers of Mistwood (Level 1-2)

**Village Immersion:** Fog-parted trail reveals Mistwood—thatch huts woven into colossal oaks, lanterns like fireflies, elves/humans in harmony (yet tense).

- **Welcoming Suspicion:** Elder Mira greets warily; roleplay feast (Insight DC 14: detect Garrick's bullying glare). Accidental power surge during meal (e.g., PC's spell misfires spectacularly—harmless but ominous).
- **Blight Boar Incursion:** Massive tainted boar (CR 1, +necrotic aura) rampages market. Victory yields corrupted thorn (Arcana DC 13: Void-tainted).
- **Night Watch:** Ghosts of shipwreck haunt dreams (Wisdom save DC 12 or exhaustion level 1). **Level Up:** To 2 after boar. **Treasure:** Village herbalist's gift—2 potions of healing.

##### Act 3: Thorne's Hidden Grove (Level 2)

**Wild Trek:** Lirael guides through mist-choked woods (3-hour journey: random encounters—illusionary pitfalls, DC 13 Intelligence saves).

- **En Route Hazards:** Blighted twig blights (CR 1/8 x10) swarm; fey pixies offer riddles for safe passage (riddle success: minor illusion boon).
- **Hermit Meet:** Thorne (reclusive druid, wood elf elder) in camouflaged glade. Lore dump: Heartstone guards veil; Blight from shattered core. Assigns "prove worth" fetch: rare moonbloom herb.
- **Roleplay Depth:** Thorne probes amnesias (Medicine/Insight DC 14: PC backstory flashes). **Treasure:** Thorne's staff (druidic focus, _entangle_ 1/day).

##### Act 4: Sylva's Peril (Level 2)

**Rescue Quest:** Herb hunt leads to vine-trapped Sylva (scout NPC, entangled by Blight assassin vines—CR 1/2).

- **Vine Labyrinth:** Dexterity skill challenge (DC 14, 4 successes); combat: 2 vine blights + 1 shambling mound sapling (CR 1 scaled).
- **Bonding Moment:** Freed Sylva joins (ranged support, lore on Blight cults). Shares tale: Her kin fell to shadows.
- **Awakening Tease:** Mists trigger group vision—party glimpses shared fate (e.g., "Four lights against the Void"). **Level Up:** None. **Treasure:** Sylva's cloak of elvenkind.

##### Act 5: Sacred Awakening (Level 2-3)

**Grove Ritual:** Return to Thorne's; communal rite around standing stones (group Nature/Arcana DC 15 challenge).

- **Power Surge:** Each PC gains custom boon (e.g., Fighter: temp HP on kills; Wizard: extra cantrip). Dramatic manifestations (fireball mishap singes trees).
- **Intruder Alarm:** Blight sprites (reskinned imps, CR 1) spy; chase yields map fragment to ruins.
- **Foreshadow Betrayal:** Lirael confides: Village whispers of "shadow faithful." **Level Up:** To 3 post-ritual. **Treasure:** Fey rune ring (detect magic 3/day).

##### Act 6: Mistwood's Shadow (Level 3)

**Return & Betrayal:** Village under cultist siege (Garrick leads 4 cult fanatics + 2 blighted zombies, CR 3 deadly).

- **Moral Choices:** Evacuate families (Athletics DC 15) or pursue Garrick (Stealth DC 14). Discover hidden shrine with Void idol.
- **Interrogation:** Captured cultist spills: Morva the hag directs from ruins.
- **Immersive Tension:** Villagers divide—some hail PCs as saviors, others fear "outsider magic." **Treasure:** Cultist hoard—200 gp, scroll of _protection from evil_.

##### Act 7: Morva's Deception (Level 3-4)

**Ruins Delve:** Overgrown Netherese-style dungeon (5 rooms: traps like glyph of warding DC 14, puzzles—align runes DC 16).

- **Encounters:** Animated armors (CR 1 x4), Morva (night hag variant, CR 4; bargains secrets for blood oaths—Wisdom DC 16 resist).
- **Hag's Lair:** Cryptic prophecies ("Heart calls the lost"); reveals Void Whisperer's name. Moral: Free imprisoned fey or sacrifice for power?
- **Clue Harvest:** Heartstone map shard points to volcanic core. **Level Up:** To 4 after hag. **Treasure:** Hag's eye gem (_scrying_ 1/week).

##### Act 8: Heartstone Reckoning (Level 4-5)

**Core Descent:** Lava-veined tunnels to chamber (environmental: heat exhaustion DC 15 Con hourly).

- **Guardian Trials:** 3 phases—earth elemental (CR 5), shadow swarm (CR 4), rift puzzle (Intelligence DC 17 seal nodes).
- **Climax:** Void Whisperer (shadow demon + lair actions: Blight tentacles; CR 5 deadly). Phases: Minions, corrupting aura (Wisdom saves), purify Heartstone (group Arcana DC 18).
- **Resolution:** Blight recedes; memories surge. Lirael seals rift (sacrifice option: she fades heroically). Party hailed as Mistwalkers. **Level Up:** To 5 on victory. **Treasure:** Heartstone Shard Amulet (attune: resistance to necrotic, _commune with nature_ 1/day).

#### Key Adaptations & Immersive Enhancements

- **Pacing:** 8 acts = granular progression; each ~1-2 sessions with breathing room for roleplay/exploration.
- **Immersion Boost:** Mist tables (roll d6: 1-2 illusions, 3-4 whispers, 5-6 boons); personalized visions tie to backstories.
- **Combat Variety:** 60% environmental (mists, vines), 40% bosses; short rests encouraged.
- **DM Tips:** Track "Mist Affinity" (acts unlock boons). Use handouts: Faded maps, prophecy scraps.

# **Campaign Overview: Echoes of the Seven Harmonies

This D&D 5e campaign (2014 core rules) for **4 level 5 adventurers** (progressing to level 10) spans **14-18 immersive sessions**, expanding the urgency of healing Eldrath through the **Seven Harmonies**—ancient druidic songs embodying elemental and spiritual essences (Gale/Wind, Ember/Fire, Tide/Water, Root/Growth, Gleam/Light, Depths/Shadow, Crag/Earth). Each Harmony must be mastered (its "soul" discovered via trials) before a lunar month's end, or Lirael's fading spirit dissipates permanently, and the reforged Heartstone shatters, merging Eldrath with the Shadowfell.

The mists thin dangerously, spawning Void echoes (corrupting storms, illusions). **Grimthar the Stormreaver**—a colossal ogre mage (ogre mage stats enhanced: petrifying gaze, Blight aura, legendary actions summoning minions)—hoards stolen essences in his chasm citadel, seeking to twist the songs into a dirge of destruction. Sylva returns as full NPC ally (ranger, levels with party). Lirael's spirit manifests in visions, granting cryptic guidance but growing translucent.

**Immersive Elements:**

- **Urgency Timer:** 28 in-game days (tracked via phases: waxing moon = strengthening mists; waning = weakening wards). Each act consumes 3-5 days.
- **Song Mastery:** Group learns via collaborative rituals (Performance/Nature DC 16-20); boons stack (e.g., Gale: _gust of wind_ shared).
- **Mist Effects:** Random d6 table (1-2: disorienting fog; 3-4: prophetic whispers; 5-6: temporary boon).
- **Themes:** Heart over hubris; interdependence; seeing with the soul (Insight/Perception checks emphasize intuition).

- **Mastery:** Boon: _create or destroy water_ + swim speed. **Treasure:** Tidal pearl (water breathing 1/hour).

##### Act 5: Song of the Root (Growth) – Verdant Labyrinth (Level 6-7)

**Ancient Forest Maze:** Awakened plants, assassin vines (Dex DC 17).

- **Growth Trial:** Plant a seed of hope (Nature DC 19); combat: shambling mound + blights (CR 8).

##### Act 6: Songs of Gleam & Depths (Light & Shadow) – Fey Glades & Blighted Chasms (Level 7)

**Dual Paths:** Split party briefly (or parallel); mirror mazes + will-o'-wisps (CR 8).

- **Gleam Trial:** Dispel shadow illusions (Perception DC 20); light spirit grants truth vision.
- **Depths Trial:** Redeem shadowed fey (moral combat vs. wights, CR 8); temper darkness.
- **Midpoint Crisis:** Grimthar's raid on neutral site; ambush (CR 9: ogre + minions). **Treasure:** Gleam lens (true seeing 1/short rest).

##### Act 7: Song of the Crag (Earth) – Crystal Caverns (Level 7-8)

**Earthquake Depths:** Cave-ins (Dex DC 18); align resonating crystals (Arcana DC 20).

- **Earth Elemental Guardians:** Combat + puzzle (CR 9); forge alliance with dao-like spirits.
- **Final Harmony:** Group weaves all songs (Performance DC 21); combo effects unlock.
- **Grimthar's Taunt:** Sending vision; offers corrupted shortcut. **Level Up:** To 8. **Treasure:** Crag stone (earth resistance + _spike growth_).

##### Act 8: Reforging Symphony (Level 8-10)

**Chasm Assault:** Multi-room citadel (traps: petrifying gaze rays, Con DC 17).

- **Minion Waves:** Blighted giants + shadows (CR 9-10).
- **Boss Phases:** Use songs to counter lair actions; **Grimthar** (CR 12 enhanced: HP 250+, legendary actions summon Blight, gaze petrifies).
- **Resolution:** Defeat yields stolen essences; full chorus at Heartstone altar reforges it. Lirael stabilizes (permanent ally: _commune with nature_ 1/week). Visions of deeper threats. **Level Up:** To 9 mid-dungeon, 10 on victory. **Treasure:** Harmony Flute (any learned song 1/day), epic hoard.

#### Key Adaptations & Immersive Enhancements

- **Combat Variety:** 50% environmental (winds, lava, vines), 50% bosses; short rests in sanctuaries.

**Elowen** remains as prophetic ally (now with greater divination powers); **Lirien**, the graceful deer-spirit satyr (satyr + druid spells), joins as the emotional anchor and symbol of genuine connection. But this awakening also tears open massive aerial rifts in the sky above Eldrath, unleashing tempests, thunder elementals, and Void-tainted storm horrors that threaten to rip the entire isle into the elemental chaos of the sky or hurl it into the Shadowfell abyss.

The mastermind behind this cataclysm is **Rhivak the Stormreaver**, a colossal storm giant warlord (kraken sovereign base stats, heavily enhanced: winged flight, thunder/lightning breath, legendary actions summoning storm minions, lair actions creating vortexes and lightning grids). He commands a floating citadel in the storm-swept spires and seeks to shatter the Heartstone once and for all, using its power to ascend as an eternal storm god and consume Eldrath.

**Aeloria**, a young bronze dragon in hawk-spirit form (young bronze dragon stats with oracle abilities), joins as the final ally and symbol of hope. All previous companions (Sylva, Elowen, Lirien) return, now fully scaled and integrated into the party’s support structure.

**Immersive Elements:**

- **Storm Countdown:** 14 in-game days until the **Night of the Longest Tempest**, when Rhivak plans to complete his ritual and tear Eldrath apart.
- **Storm Effects:** d6 table (1-2: lightning strikes; 3-4: wind shear; 5-6: temporary storm resistance).
- **Wing Attunement:** Trials grant progressive flight and storm mastery; final wings are permanent and legendary.
- **Themes:** Unity above all; the cost of true power; ascension through sacrifice; becoming more than mortal.

#### 8-Act Structure: The Skyward Ascension

##### Act 1: The Sky Splits (Level 18)

**Hook:** The Heartstone blooms with blinding light; sky rifts tear open above Mistwood, dropping lightning and thunder elementals. Lirael’s spirit proclaims: “Wings await the worthy in the storm spires. Unite the winds’ children ere the longest night, or Eldrath is cast into the void.”

- **Skyfall Defense:** Repel mariliths riding storm drakes + wind elementals (CR 18 deadly).
- **Aeloria’s Descent:** The young dragon crashes through a rift, wounded; party saves her and she joins.
- **Prophecy Map:** Runes reveal the four major factions and their sky sanctuaries. **Treasure:** Aeloria’s scale (resistance to lightning/thunder 1/hour).

##### Act 2: Trial of the First Wing – Vortex Spires (Level 18)

**Thunder Peaks:** Vertical climb/fly through wind-carved spires (Athletics/Fly DC 23 group challenge).

- **Air Elemental Conclave:** Appease or defeat elder air elementals (CR 18).
- **Wing Shard:** First permanent wing fragment (fly speed 60 ft. for one PC).
- **Grim Portent:** Rhivak’s harpy choir broadcasts a taunting ultimatum across the island. **Treasure:** Stormtalon bracers (fly 80 ft. burst 1/day).

##### Act 3: The Feywind Exiles – Floating Isles (Level 18-19)

**Skyborne Glades:** Navigate levitating islands linked by wind bridges.

- **Archfey Council:** Roleplay negotiation + illusion storm combat (CR 19).
- **Harmony Integration:** Weave all previous songs into an aerial rite (Performance DC 24).
- **Mastery:** Boon: group fly speed 60 ft. when together. **Level Up:** To 19. **Treasure:** Wind harmony feather (group flight advantage).

##### Act 4: The Earthbound Ancients – Crag Vortices (Level 19)

**Floating Stone Rings:** Earthquake-riddled aerial platforms.

- **Purple Worm & Stone Guardians:** Combat + quake traps (CR 19).
- **Stonewing Ritual:** Fuse earth essence into wings (Nature DC 24).
- **Soul Test:** One PC must sacrifice a treasured item to prove humility. **Treasure:** Crag anchor (earth resistance + feather fall at will).

##### Act 5: The Blight-Touched Spirits – Shadow Vortices (Level 19)

**Dark Storm Clouds:** Navigate corrupted thunderheads.

- **Redeem or Destroy:** Moral combat against wraiths and shadow dragons (CR 19).
- **Umbral Lift:** Gain anti-gravity wing aspect.
- **Midpoint Assault:** Rhivak sends death knights on nightmare pegasi (CR 19 raid). **Treasure:** Shadow wing cloak (fly in darkness).

##### Act 6: The Flame Remnants – Ash Squalls (Level 19)

**Burning Storm Fronts:** Fire-air elemental clashes.

- **Efreeti Warlords:** Epic combat + forge alliance (CR 19).
- **Inferno Glide:** Final wing aspect (fire immunity during flight).
- **Aeloria’s Vision:** She foresees “one must fall to raise all.” **Treasure:** Flame storm gem (fire/lightning damage boost).

##### Act 7: The Grand Conclave (Level 19-20)

**Central Spire Summit:** All factions gather for the final wing-forging ritual.

- **Group Ritual:** Full chorus of songs + all wing shards (Performance/Arcana DC 25).
- **Complete Stormwings:** Permanent fly speed 90 ft., storm resistance, legendary boons for all.
- **Rhivak’s Ultimatum:** He appears in vision, demanding surrender before the longest night. **Level Up:** To 20. **Treasure:** Oracle’s Gaze (true seeing at will).

##### Act 8: The Battle of the Longest Night (Level 20)

**Rhivak’s Floating Citadel:** Epic aerial dungeon (7 massive platforms: wind shears Dex DC 25, lightning grids).

- **Legion Waves:** Harpy choirs, hezrou swarms, giant eagles (CR 20 deadly).
- **Lieutenant Bosses:** Use faction alliances to counter specialized threats.
- **Final Boss: Rhivak the Stormreaver** (CR 23 enhanced: HP 550+, thunder breath 20d10, legendary actions summon vortexes/rifts, vulnerable to radiant/harmony damage).
- **Climax Sacrifice:** One PC channels the full Heartstone (Con DC 25: death or steal legendary action). Victory seals rifts.
- **Resolution:** Rhivak defeated; Eldrath stabilized. Wings become eternal. Companions ascend or remain as guardians. Lirael merges fully into the Heartstone, granting _wish_-like communion once per month. **Treasure:** Heartstone Wings (attunement: permanent fly 120 ft., control weather 1/day, epic boons), colossal hoard (50,000+ gp, legendary artifacts).

#### Key Adaptations & Immersive Enhancements

- **Pacing:** 8 acts = grand escalation from individual trials to unified epic battle.
- **Immersion Boost:** Vertical 3D combat descriptions; storm soundscapes; faction roleplay depth.
- **Combat Variety:** 70% aerial/environmental, 30% titanic bosses; flight mandatory (early temp wings provided).
- **DM Tips:** Emphasize verticality, weather effects, and personal sacrifice moments for maximum emotional impact.

#### Epilogue Hook: Guardians Beyond the Veil

With Eldrath saved, the Heartstone blooms into a radiant world-tree that pierces the mists, creating stable portals to other planes. Lirael’s final whisper echoes: “The veil thins eternal. Shadows beyond hunger for the isle’s heart.” Distant rifts flicker with unknown threats—setting the stage for a level 20+ epilogue campaign defending Eldrath against multiversal invaders, planar gods, or the return of ancient forces from Toril’s deepest history.

The tale of the Mistwalkers is complete. Eldrath endures—because four wanderers became its eternal wings.
