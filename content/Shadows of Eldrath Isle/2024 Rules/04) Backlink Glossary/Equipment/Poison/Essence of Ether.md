#### Essence of Ether [[Essence of Ether|(300 GP)]]
*Inhaled Poison*

A creature subjected to Essence of Ether must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or have the [[Poisoned]] condition for 8 hours. The creature also has the [[Unconscious]] condition while Poisoned
in this way. The creature wakes up if it takes damage or if another creature takes an action to shake it awake.
