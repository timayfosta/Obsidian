#### Orc Traits
**Creature Type:** Humanoid
**Size:** Medium (about 6 - 7 feet tall)
**Speed:** 30 feet

As an Orc, you have these special traits.

***Adrenaline Rush.*** You can take the [[Dash]] action as a Bonus Action. When you do so, you gain a number of [[Temporary Hit Points]] equal to your Proficiency Bonus. You can use this trait a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a [[Short Rest|Short]] or [[Long Rest]].

***Darkvision.*** You have [[Darkvision]] with a range of 120 feet.
